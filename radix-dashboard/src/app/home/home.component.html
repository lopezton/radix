Welcome to Radix!

What is Radix?

Radix is the home portal application for Xpanxion. The goal of Radix is to centralize important information and tools to help better organize our employees and daily activities.


Feedback?

Is something broken? Want to see a new feature? We'd love to hear from you. Send an email to ADMIN with more information so we can improve Radix right away!


Want to Contribute?

For the people and by the people. Xpanxion Radix is developed in-house and is a great stomping ground for employees wishing to obtain real-world software development experience. Send an email to LEAD_DEV and join the Radix team!
